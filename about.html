TriContinent Trio

We are a group of three students from Futuregames Stockholm's Frontend Developer program, exploring the world of web development. For this project, we fetched data from three different APIs to create an interactive movie night idea generator. We also focused on building a responsive design to reflect modern UX best practices and maintained a clear Git workflow to ensure smooth collaboration and efficient development.
